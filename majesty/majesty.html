Chef Message

We aim to home-produce as much as possiblefor the best quality,
and to reduce food miles. Our delicious cakes, traditional Devon
scones, breads, soups, sauces and accompaniments are produced
in our own kitchens – and we use herbs from our gardens when in
season. Even our bottled water is produced in-house, using a
sophisticated seven-stage filtration.
